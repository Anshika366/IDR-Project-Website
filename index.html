Bridging Academy & Industry

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber, and technology risk roles within regulated, high-consequence environments.

Our unique model emphasizes the link between academic insight (UK University partnerships) and real-world projects, ensuring our candidates are ready for the most demanding technical challenges.
